Sell Your Magic: The Gathering Cards

Honest offers. Fast communication. No pressure.

How It Works

Whether you have a shoebox of cards or a full collection, I make the process simple and transparent:

  • 1. Send me photos of your cards, boxes, or binders.
  • 2. I give you a fair offer based on condition, playability, and current market value.
  • 3. We meet locally (Brainerd area) or arrange shipping if needed.
  • 4. You get paid via cash, PayPal, or Venmo.

What I Buy

  • Commander staples and playables
  • Bulk commons/uncommons (sorted or unsorted)
  • Rares and mythics
  • Full collections
  • Binders, decks, and loose boxes
